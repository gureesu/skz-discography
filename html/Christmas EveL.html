Christmas EveL

11-19-2021

Christmas Evel is Stray Kids' first-ever holiday release. The mini-album consists of four tracks, including the dual lead singles "Christmas EveL", and "Winter Falls", with a theme of "Christmas naughty boy" and a Christmas, hip hop, and pop sound.

In traditional Stray Kids fashion, the title track is very upbeat and lively however the lyrics in this song are quite pessimistic.

Instead of expressing happiness over the well-beloved holiday, the members sing and rap about the rather apathetic and negative emotions that come with the winter season.
