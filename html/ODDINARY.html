ODDINARY

03-18-2022

“MANIAC” is an influential untamed trap electronic-pop song that tells the story of how normally “odd” people, will be not be seen as ordinary and will stay “weird” but different from others.

The song shares the similar electronic-pop genre as their previous hit releases, such as “神메뉴 (God’s Menu)” and “소리꾼 (Thunderous)”, both of which achieved massive success for their different sounds.

The music video showcases Stray Kids' fun-yet-powerful selves with gothic and horrifying scenes. The members can also be seen playing with powers in a variety of locations, similar to their previous music video for “소리꾼 (Thunderous)”.
