SKZ-REPLAY

12-21-2022

SKZ-Replay is the third compilation album by Stray Kids. The album consists of 25 tracks split into two sides: the A-side contains ten songs, eight performed solo and two performed by the group as a whole, and the B-side includes original songs from their video series SKZ-Player and SKZ-Record.

As a "self-produced group", all eight members of Stray Kids have worked on and recorded their own solo material, reaffirming their broad spectrum and revealing their distinct personalities and authenticity to music.

Source: Genius.com, Stray-kids.fandom.com
