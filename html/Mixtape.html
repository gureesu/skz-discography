Mixtape

01-08-2018

“Mixtape” is the pre-debut EP from JYPE K-pop group, Stray Kids. The EP consists of 7 songs which were recorded and performed by the group on the reality TV Show “JYP’s Stray Kids” as challenges for the debut-member candidates.

The EP includes the highly successful single, ‘Hellavator", and debuted at #2 on Billboard’s World Albums chart. It also landed within Top 10 of various iTunes Album Charts across the globe.

The EP was released alongside the performance video “Beware”. The performance video of “Spread My Wings” was uploaded online a week later.
