Clé 1: MIROH

03-25-2019

Clé 1 : MIROH is the 4th mini-album by the South Korean boy group Stray Kids.

The word Clé means key in French, while MIROH sounds phonetically like ‘maze’ in Korean, giving the literal translation to the album name is “Key 1 : Maze”.

In this mini-album, each track has the courage and challenge of Stray Kids, so you can see not only musical changes but also the boys' growth.

The group also sings about passion, heartbreak, boyhood, adulthood, and another first step in life.
