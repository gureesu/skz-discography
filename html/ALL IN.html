ALL IN

10-27-2020

‘ALL IN’ is the first single and title track of Stray Kids' first Japanese mini-album. The release showcases a rebellious and explosive sound with powerful brass influences that drive the song.

This confident style shines through within the lyrics, the artists asserting their influence as they surpass those who challenge them and rebound from obstacles that threaten their progress.
