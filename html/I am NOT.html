I am NOT

03-26-2018

“I am NOT” is the debut EP by K-pop group, Stray Kids. The EP is the highly anticipated follow-up to their “Mixtape” released two months prior.

The EP was very well received by international fans. With the EP, Stray Kids has claimed the top spot on the iTunes Album Chart in 10 different countries. All tracks were composed and written by members of the group.

The title track ‘District 9’ broke the record for the most views a K-pop group debut music video has achieved in the first 24 hours.
