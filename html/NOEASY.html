NOEASY

08-23-2021

“소리꾼 (Thunderous),” the title track from Stray Kids' second full studio album, NOEASY, is a decisive clap-back at those who have criticized the group for their ‘noisy’ sound, and a triumphant return to the spotlight after taking home the trophy from Mnet’s Kingdom: Legendary War competition.

The track is layered with samples of car horns and other effects that blend seamlessly with traditional Korean flutes and thunderous drums, while the lyrics pay homage to Korean legends and paint the group as a new legend in their own right.
