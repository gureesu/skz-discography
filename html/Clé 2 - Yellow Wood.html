Clé 2: Yellow Wood

06-19-2019

Clé 2 : Yellow Wood is the second album in the “Clé” series by Stray Kids following Clé 1 : MIROH.

The title “Yellow Wood” is taken from the first line of Robert Frost’s famous poem “The Road Not Taken” which depicts the emotions that are present when faced with a choice that could alter your future path completely.

This mini-album also includes the four “Mixtape” tracks digitally for the first time, that were featured on Stray Kids' first four releases since their debut. Originally, the tracks were a “CD Only” track that was not released online.
