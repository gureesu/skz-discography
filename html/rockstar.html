樂-STAR

11-10-2023

ROCK-STAR (樂-STAR) is the eighth mini album of Stray Kids. The title track "LALALALA" is a song that excites listeners through its unique melody, delivering fresh stimulation with lyrics that feature wordplay utilizing 'rock'.

In addition, by adding rock elements to the repeated sound in the chorus, you can enjoy the powerful addictive and intense nature of Stray Kids' music.

The group's confidence to create music that can be enjoyed by all listeners around the world is evident in the album's bold message, "No matter what, our rock continues." The album is a celebration of those who can enjoy themselves.
