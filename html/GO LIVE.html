GO 生

06-17-2020

Stray Kids' first full album, GO生, debuted on June 17th, 2020. The album title includes the Chinese Hanja character “生” which means “to live/life” so the album is named as a call-to-action to “GO LIVE.”

Additionally, the lead single “神메뉴 (God’s Menu)” combines the Chinese Hanja character “神” meaning “God” with the Korean transliteration of the word “Menu” (메뉴). The concept for the album came from the title track and Stray Kids released a track list that featured special “ingredients” for each track.

The album includes 10 new tracks as well as 4 tracks that were released through previous single projects (TOP, SLUMP, Mixtape: Gone Days, Mixtape: On Track).
