MAXIDENT

10-07-2022

Stray Kids' wholesome lead single “CASE 143” charms its way into their listeners' hearts as the song opens their seventh mini-album, MAXIDENT.

The song consists of Stray Kids' signature dynamic electronic dance pop-style. The song’s lyrics—written by the group’s production team, 3RACHA—express the uncontrollable feeling of attraction towards someone, but not being able to express their feelings.

This is why instead of just saying “l love you,” they use the numeric code instead (I (1), love (4), you (3)). 143 is a slang term that originated in the 1990s as a code back when pagers were popular.
