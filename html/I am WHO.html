I am WHO

08-16-2018

“My Pace” is the second track of South Korean boy group Stray Kids' second official EP, I am WHO.

It is a mashup of pop and hip-hop with strong electronic elements, underscored by throbbing bass and booming horns.

Accompanying this is some brisk drumming, a springy guitar riff and even a light piano melody to tie together this fun, catchy piece.

The album in particular expands on this by demonstrating how this anxiety blossoms into self-doubt in the wake of others that are perceived as better.
