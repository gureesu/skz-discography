06-02-2023

5-STAR is the third full length album by Stray Kids. Released on June 2, 2023, it contains 12 songs, including the title track “S Class” and other songs such as “Hall of Fame”, “Collision”, “FNF”, “Super Bowl”, and “Youtiful”. The album also includes the Korean version of “The Sound”.

The album contains uplifting and powerful themes found in songs like “FNF” (a tribute to all the flora and fauna lost in the Australian wildfires), “Collision” (which compares the love between two people to the attraction between two planets) and “Youtiful” (which celebrates the uniqueness of every person and comforts listeners, giving them enough strength to smile and believe in ourselves).
