I am YOU

10-22-2018

Stray Kids' I am YOU is a mini-album that contains 8 tracks, including title song “I am YOU.”

It is an album that depicts the uneasiness of youth, but ‘I’ grows through YOU – a touching, hopeful message.

The group has participated in the song “I am YOU,” followed by their debut album Mixtape, their official debut mini album I am NOT and their mini album I am WHO presenting themselves as singer-songwriter idols.
