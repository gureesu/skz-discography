CIRCUS

06-22-2022

In the world of “CIRCUS” Stray Kids welcome the audience into their circus show filled with fire rings, cannons, wrappers, and captivating visuals. The song is accompanied with a noise-pop and electronic-pop following the dynamic songs that Stray Kids produce to their fans.

The song’s official music video showcases Stray Kids in a circus set where it shows both behind-the-scenes of their circus shows, and the variety of exciting surprises in their circus.

“CIRCUS” is the title track of 2nd Japanese mini-album named the same title. The song is a pre-release single that also came out with the Japanese version of “MANIAC” and “Your Eyes”.
