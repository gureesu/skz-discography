Clé : LEVANTER

12-09-2019

The book ‘The Alchemist’ has been a major influence in this comeback, especially in the title track: 바람 (Levanter). The Levanter in the book is a powerful wind that symbolises change and/or transformation. Therefore the song itself also represents a certain change Stray Kids is facing.

"Levanter" is about letting go of something — of a dream, a feeling, or even a person — but it isn't a sad song. In fact, it's full of hope and possibility, and with the awareness that the path is ultimately theirs to define.
